From the golden shoreline of the Mediterranean to the picturesque, cool mountain climes, Provence is set to offer even the most experienced travellers a tale of the unexpected.

Its Côte d'Azur is studded with famed starlets, celebrity hangouts and a veritable millionaire’s row of old-age glamour and excess. Yet along the sublime coastline you’ll find secluded bays, gloriously quaint fishing villages, and unsung towns like Bandol and Menton. Head inland, for enchanting medieval cities, famed wine regions and the glorious, softly-scented purple of Provence’s iconic lavender fields.

Discover the art of living in Provence, where the famed light illuminates the technicolour produce of the region’s beloved food markets, where the welcome is warm, friendly and sincere and the living is good, easy, and irresistible to any traveller.
